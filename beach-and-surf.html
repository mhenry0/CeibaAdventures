What to Expect

Chase perfect swells, sun-kissed sand, and laid-back coastal vibes on Costa Rica’s legendary South-Pacific breaks. You’ll meet our friendly guides at 7 AM in Uvita or Dominical, then unwind on the scenic drive past palm-lined beaches before arriving at your surf playground.

Once there, enjoy a full safety briefing and personalized coaching from our ISA-qualified instructors. We provide high-performance surfboards, rash-guards, and all the gear you need—no experience required, just a sense of adventure!

At midday, refuel with a beach barbecue lunch featuring fresh ceviche, tropical fruits, and cold refreshments. Between sets, keep an eye out for playful dolphins offshore or scarlet macaws flying overhead.

As the sun dips toward the horizon, capture your best waves with our optional photo package, then relax on the golden sand before we return you to your hotel by sunset—memories (and a healthy tan) included.

✔️ What’s Included
  • Round-trip transport

  • Certified ISA surf coach
